# Research Summary — Intelligent Automation Wins in Enterprise AI Adoption
**Date**: 2026-03-20
**Sources**: 30 verified (see source_verification.md)
**Coverage**: 2024–2026

---

## Core Thesis
Intelligent automation has crossed from experimental to operational in 2024–2026, delivering measurable, documented outcomes across financial services, insurance, manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare. The organisations achieving the greatest impact share one characteristic: they redesigned workflows around automation rather than layering AI onto unchanged processes. The gap between high performers and the majority is not about model capability — it is about data quality, integration discipline, and governance maturity.

## Definitional Note: Intelligent Automation
For this pipeline, "intelligent automation" encompasses:
1. **Robotic Process Automation (RPA)** — rules-based workflow automation (UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism)
2. **AI-augmented automation** — ML/NLP layered on RPA for judgment-based tasks (JPMorgan COiN, Allianz)
3. **Generative AI workflow tools** — LLM-based productivity automation (Microsoft 365 Copilot, BBVA ChatGPT Enterprise)
4. **Agentic AI** — autonomous agents capable of multi-step reasoning and action (Allianz agentic claims, Salesforce Agentforce, Walmart supply chain agents)
5. **Industrial AI** — AI embedded in physical/operational systems (Siemens Industrial Copilot, Amazon robotics, UPS ORION)
